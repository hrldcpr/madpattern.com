MadPattern is a collection of Adobe Illustrator templates

compatible with cs4 and cs5

  • one template for each type of pattern
  • automatic symmetric replication
  • automatic clipping of elements drawn "out of bounds"
  • easy to save/export repeating tile (for webpage backgrounds)
...by the way, they're free to download

a few years ago i started investigating patterns. i couldn't find any tools that would let me design them in the way that i wanted, so i started trying to make illustrator do it.

i found that the templates made it incredibly easy to quickly learn about and generate really cool patterns. once you've made some cool patterns, upload the tiles you create to the flickr group and they'll appear in the gallery for all to see.
